Our Story

Born from the heart of Maharashtra, SugranSakhi Homemade brings you the authentic flavors that have been passed down through generations. Our journey began in a small kitchen where love for traditional Indian cooking met the desire to share these precious recipes with the world.

Every dish we create is a celebration of our rich cultural heritage, made with the same care and attention that our grandmothers put into their cooking. From the aromatic spices to the perfectly balanced flavors, each bite tells a story of tradition, love, and authenticity.
